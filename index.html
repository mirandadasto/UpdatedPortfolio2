About Me

Hello, my name is Miranda D'Asto. I am currently working on my bachelors for IT management. I am on track to graduate in April of 2021. In the meantime, I am taking a course in coding and full-stack web development offered by the University of Utah to supplement my education. My life can get a little crazy as I have a full-time job and am attending two different schools. I can honestly say I'm never bored!

In my free time, I enjoy going to my studio where I train on aerial apparatuses such as hammock, lyra (hoop), and silks. I have a performance in February that I train for in the evenings and on the weekends. There is never a free moment, but six months from now, I'll be so very happy I did what I did! I have also started taking horse back riding lessons (again) on the weekends. I lead an exciting life full of education and adventure! I am very happy where I am, but so very excited for my future.
